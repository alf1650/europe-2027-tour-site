## Recommended Story Structure for the Site

1. Hero: "Europe 2027 Gathering Tour"
2. Timeline strip: all flight legs + date markers
3. Destination chapters:
   - Brussels (arrival + city highlights)
   - Slovenia (nature + day trips)
   - London (final city stop)
4. Gallery by theme:
   - City Icons
  - Gathering Moments
   - Food
   - Scenic/Outdoor
5. Practical references:
   - Airport transfer notes
  - Group split-plan note for schedule flexibility
   - Return-flight checklist
